##  Role Responsibilities

###  QA Owner

The **QA** is responsible for:

-  **Maintain documentation** - Keep all test docs current and accurate
-  **Ensure coverage visibility** - Monitor and report test 
-  **Drive quarterly review** - Schedule and facilitate regular documentation reviews
-  **Identify gaps** - Highlight areas needing attention or improvement
-  **Report on quality metrics** - Provide regular quality status updates
